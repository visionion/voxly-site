Features

Global Hotkey

Press ⌘⇧Space anywhere to start dictating. Works system-wide, in any app.

Live Transcription

See your words appear as you speak — no waiting until the end.

Direct Text Insertion

Text is automatically typed at your cursor position. No copy-paste needed.

Push-to-Talk or Toggle

Hold to record and release, or press once to start and again to stop.

Multiple AI Models

Installing

  1. Open Voxly.dmg and drag Voxly into your Applications folder.
  2. Because Voxly is a free app distributed outside the App Store, macOS may say it "is damaged" on first launch. This is just Gatekeeper — to allow it, open Terminal and run:
    xattr -dr com.apple.quarantine /Applications/Voxly.app
    Then open Voxly normally.
  3. Grant Microphone and Accessibility permissions when prompted — these let Voxly hear you and type at your cursor. Nothing leaves your Mac.
